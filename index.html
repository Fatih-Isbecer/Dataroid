About Dataroid

Dataroid is an AI-powered enterprise-level digital analytics platform that helps enterprises to enhance every individual customer experience through deep customer insight, data science modelling, and omnichannel marketing.

The platform enables digital product owners and marketers to collect and analyze real-time customer interactions from web, mobile, and other customer channels, view unified user profiles, and create and distribute personalized messages across multiple engagement channels.

How Dataroid Works with Google Cloud

1

Data Collection & Ingestion

Customer interactions from web, mobile, and digital channels are collected in real-time and streamed to Google Cloud via Pub/Sub for immediate processing.

2

Unified Customer Profiles

BigQuery processes and unifies data into comprehensive customer profiles, enabling a 360-degree view of user behavior across all touchpoints.

3

AI-Powered Analytics

Google Cloud AI services analyze customer data to generate predictive insights, segmentation models, and lifetime value forecasts.

4

Personalized Engagement

Cloud Functions trigger personalized omnichannel campaigns based on real-time insights, delivering relevant messages via email, push, SMS, and more.

5

Performance Optimization

Continuous analysis of campaign performance with real-time dashboards, leveraging Google Cloud's scalable infrastructure for always-on availability.
